# AXIOM LEDGER (v51)

This ledger lists every axiom that any Lean declaration in this package
depends on, as reported by `#print axioms` (see `scripts/PrintAxioms.lean`,
output captured verbatim in `docs/axioms.txt`). It is regenerated by
`scripts/rebuild.sh` on every build and should never be hand-edited.

## Core (kernel/logic) axioms

These three are standard Lean 4 core axioms, not physics posits. Any of the
headline (Bucket A) theorems may depend on some subset of them and this is
considered a clean result.

| Axiom | Role |
|---|---|
| `propext` | propositional extensionality (standard) |
| `Quot.sound` | quotient soundness (standard) |
| `Classical.choice` | classical choice (standard; used only by `finiteCertificateClosureComplete`, via `Classical.byContradiction`) |

## Named physics-posit axioms: NONE in the headline build

v50 declared two global axioms, `pairResolution : Prop` /
`pairResolution_holds : pairResolution` and `physicalOperativity : Prop` /
`physicalOperativity_holds : physicalOperativity`, whose sole purpose was to
manufacture proof inhabitants for `secondOrderResult`. v51 removes both
axioms entirely (see `AflbLean/Posits.lean`).

In their place:

- The decidable part of `hyp:pair-resolution` (coverage + exclusivity of the
  threshold classifier) is now a **genuine theorem**: `Posits.classify`,
  `Posits.classify_covers`, `Posits.classify_exclusive`. No axiom needed.
- The non-decidable "intrinsicity" claim and `hyp:ossv5-physical-operativity`
  are **not declared in Lean at all** (no `axiom`, no `def` asserted true).
  They are threaded into `SecondOrder.secondOrderResult` as bare universally
  quantified `Prop` parameters (`pairRes physOp : Prop`) together with proof
  parameters (`_hpairRes : pairRes`, `_hphysOp : physOp`) supplied by the
  *caller*. `#print axioms AflbLean.secondOrderResult` (line 11 of
  `docs/axioms.txt`) shows only `[propext, Quot.sound]` — no axiom
  dependency on either hypothesis. The dependency is visible only in the
  theorem's own type signature, which is the honest way to represent "this
  result is conditional on an externally supplied physical hypothesis."

This eliminates the "axiom used merely to manufacture a proof inhabitant"
pattern flagged against v50.

## Legacy/ledger axioms (non-headline)

These five axioms live under `AflbLean/Legacy/` and are **not** imported by
the root `AflbLean.lean` module and **not** covered by the
headline-axiom-only check in `scripts/rebuild.sh` step 6. They are retained
unchanged from v50/v47 as an explicit, clearly-separated registration-theory
/ imported-analytic layer.

| Axiom | File | Status |
|---|---|---|
| `AflbLean.Legacy.Hypotheses.sharedBasis` | `Legacy/Hypotheses.lean` | self-referential (named posit) |
| `AflbLean.Legacy.Hypotheses.pachnerTyped` | `Legacy/Hypotheses.lean` | self-referential (named posit) |
| `AflbLean.Legacy.Hypotheses.syncResidueWellDefined` | `Legacy/Hypotheses.lean` | self-referential (named posit) |
| `AflbLean.Legacy.Imported.odeGlobalExistence` | `Legacy/Imported.lean` | self-referential ([imported] analytic claim, ledger only) |
| `AflbLean.Legacy.Imported.energyDescentIdentity` | `Legacy/Imported.lean` | self-referential ([imported] analytic claim, ledger only) |

Two conditional theorems in `Legacy/Conditional.lean` depend on these:

- `syncResidueBlindSpot` depends on `[propext, sharedBasis, syncResidueWellDefined]`.
- `topologicalClosureTwoSpaces` depends on `[pachnerTyped, sharedBasis]`.

Neither is reachable from the headline `AflbLean` root import and neither is
counted among the Bucket-A/B headline results in `CLAIM_INVENTORY.md`.

## Headline-axiom-only guarantee

`scripts/rebuild.sh` step 6 mechanically checks, for every one of the 10
Bucket-A declarations, that its line in `docs/axioms.txt` does not mention
any `AflbLean.(Posits|Physics|Closure|Fiber|SecondOrder|Legacy)`-prefixed
axiom. As of the build captured in `docs/axioms.txt`, this check passes:
every headline theorem's axiom list is a subset of
`{propext, Quot.sound, Classical.choice}`.
