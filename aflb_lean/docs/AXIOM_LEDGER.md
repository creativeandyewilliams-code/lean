## What a green build does and does not establish here

A green `lake build` together with `docs/axioms.txt` certifies that the
finite/algebraic skeleton of the physics-determination layer ‚Äî the forced
inverse-branch response sign, the coefficient-positivity keystone, sign
invariance over the surviving functional class, the fiber criterion, the
ascending-chain half of finite certificate closure, and the necessity of
viability-bearing information ‚Äî is machine-checked from the Lean kernel
using at most `propext`/`Quot.sound`, with **no physics posit** in any of
their dependency cones. It does **not** establish that `pairResolution` or
`physicalOperativity` are true of the physical world, that the ODE
underlying the energy flow actually has global solutions (asserted, not
proved, here), that any continuum/empirical reconstruction of the discrete
carrier is valid, or the finite-stabilization cardinality bound of
`thm:ossv5-finite-closure` (only the ascending-chain half is proved here;
see BUILD_REPORT.md). A green build is not a claim that the physics is
correct ‚Äî it is a claim about exactly which inferences are gap-free given
the stated axioms.
